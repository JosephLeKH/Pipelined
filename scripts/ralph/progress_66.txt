## US-665: Add type-specific icons inside Activity.jsx timeline dot indicators
**Status:** PASS
**Commit:** 821af7b

### Summary
Added imports for `Plus`, `ArrowRight`, `CalendarDays` from lucide-react direct subpath imports. Added `TYPE_ICONS` constant mapping entry types to icon components. In `TimelineEntry`, derived `DotIcon = TYPE_ICONS[entry.type]` and changed the dot span from `block h-3 w-3` to `flex h-4 w-4 items-center justify-center` with `{DotIcon && <DotIcon className="h-2 w-2 text-white/80" aria-hidden="true" />}` inside it. Unknown types get no icon (guard handles undefined).

### Learnings
- Using `{DotIcon && <DotIcon ... />}` guards against undefined icons for future/unknown entry types without needing a fallback.
- Changing `block` to `flex items-center justify-center` is the minimal change needed to center child content inside a span.
- Icon size `h-2 w-2` (8px) fits neatly inside a `h-4 w-4` (16px) dot with ring-2 accounted for.
